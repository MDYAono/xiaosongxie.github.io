Social Determinants of Health in Toronto (SDH TO)

The purpose of this web application, Social Determinants of Health in Toronto (SDH TO), is to educate and inform people of the inequality within Toronto. This is intended to be accomplished through the analysis of spatial data relating to various social determinants of health (e.g., education level, visible minority, Aboriginal identity, income level) and the distribution of COVID-19 cases.
